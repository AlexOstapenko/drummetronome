In this video I introduce the first few strokes of the frame drum. Please watch it and practice these sounds on your instrument.

First stroke - the bass sound DUM, in the text rhythm notation I will use for it letter "D".
When I say 'and', it means one pause. I denote a pause like this: '-'.

So, the first rhythm I show in the video is this:

tempo: 60 ##### instrument: Tar rhythm: D - D - D - D -

Second sound is high pitch Tak, we will use letter T. It is played with the first hand (right hand for right-handed and left for left-handed). Let's play wery slowy, alternating those two sounds: tempo: 60 ##### instrument: Tar rhythm: D - T - D - T -

The next rhythm from the video is this (follow me, now I am using pause). Click play to hear the rhythm and check how it is written:

tempo: 60 ##### instrument: Tar rhythm: D - T - D - T T D - T - D - T T
